The Game

Thanks to advanced AI systems, Neverending can automatically detect how good you are and whether someone is cheating. So you only get into lobbies that are tailored for you.

Raytracing makes graphics even more realistic, especially reflections

(later) With cross-platform game support and cloud storage, you can play anywhere on all your devices.

With the offline game mode, you can also play against bots. Thanks to the latest AI technologies, you won't feel any difference.

With first and third person mode you can play the way you want.

Why Neverending?

Its a free game so you won't lose any money
We think fair play is one of the most important things in a shooter - thats why pay2win won't work in neverending.
The community is part of the game - so that it never gets boring. This means that balancing is much quicker than with the traditional ones.
Neverending will download updates and smaller patches as you play for uninterrupted fun - so that you can also play the small 5 minute round
The community is the center of every good match - support will be always there for you
